2.3. More Modeling Examples

2.3.1. Effect of Drug on Cells in an Organ

  • $C_0$ : concentrations of the drug in the blood flowing in
  • $V$ : volume of blood in the organ
  • $N$ : Number(#) of cells in mass
  • $C_0, C(t)$ : drug concentrations

Assumptions

  1. Homogeneous (null-mixed)
    ※ Flow : 단시간 내 나가는 Volumn
  2. The outflow contains only drug ad not any cells
    • 즉, 약물의 효과만 보겠다
  3. 세포는 자동적으로 증가한다.

2.4.2 Linear Phase Planes

Let $e$-value $\lambda_1$ and $\lambda_2$ (distinct and nonzero real)

  1. both $\lambda_1, \lambda_2 < 0 \qquad \text{sink (stable node)}$
  2. $\lambda_1, \lambda_2 = 0 \qquad \qquad \text{source}$
  3. $\lambda_1, \lambda_2 >0 \qquad \qquad \text{saddle}$

Ex

Denote $v_1, v_2$ means $e$-vectors, and $\lambda_1, \lambda_2$ means $e$-values.

If $\lambda_1, \lambda_2 = a \pm ib, \ b \neq 0$

  1. $a = 0 \qquad \qquad \text{center}$
  2. $a < 0 \qquad \qquad \text{spiral sink (stable)}$
  3. $a > 0 \qquad \qquad \text{unstable}$

※ condition : All $e$-values are negative
