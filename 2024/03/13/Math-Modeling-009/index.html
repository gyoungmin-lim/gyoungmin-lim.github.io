Thm . The linear system is stable if the real point of all the $e$-values of $A$ are negative.

  • If all the $e$-values of $A$ have negative real point, conclude “ Local stability ”
    • Local stability : Every solution of $\frac{dX}{dt} = F(X)$ that starts near $X = \bar{X}$ converges to $\bar{X}$ as $t \rightarrow \infty$
